Hey, I'm Chris

I'm a detail obsessed web geek and videographer. I've spent the last three years managing spartan.com and its' 30+ international sites; collaborating with both domestic and international marketing teams, sponsors, contractors, and partners to deliver a consistent brand tone and experience for our users across the globe.

I’m passionate about data-driven web development decisions, commenting too much in my code, and generating the content that I as a daily (if not constant) user of the web, hope to see in the world.
